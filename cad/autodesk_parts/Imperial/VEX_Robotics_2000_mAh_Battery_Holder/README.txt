# Summary

Battery holder for the VEX Robotics standard 2000 mAh battery that comes with most kits.  
This was printed at:  
Standard  
4 layers  
50% infill (for rigidity of the clips)  

I went through a couple of design stages and this is the final results.  Drop me a line if you end of printing one for yourself.
